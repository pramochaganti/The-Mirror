Research Participation Agreement

Pramod Chaganti · pramod.chaganti@icloud.com

STEP 1 OF 3 — What we collect

Data collected

• Your child's daily journal entries — text they type or speak in response to one question per day.

• Your observations about your child (logged on your device separately).

• First names or nicknames only. No email, phone number, date of birth, or location is collected.

Purpose

This is a private research exercise to test whether a self-discovery tool is useful for families approaching the Class 10→11 subject choice. Findings are used solely to improve the tool. No personally identifiable information will be published or shared.

Testing phase notice

This is an early-stage test. The app may change and the exercise may be discontinued. You will be informed of any material change.

STEP 2 OF 3 — AI processing & your rights

AI processing

Each journal entry is sent to Anthropic Claude (an AI service) to identify interest patterns. The text is processed over an encrypted connection and is not stored on any server. Anthropic does not use API data for model training by default.

Your child's entries never leave their phone in full. Only derived tags (e.g. "making things") may be shared — and only if your child explicitly chooses to do so.

Storage

Child's entries: device only. Parent observations: private family database, accessible only via your family code.

Your rights under the DPDP Act 2023

Right to access — view all data within the app at any time.

Right to correction — edit any entry at any time.

Right to erasure — "Erase all data" in the Grown-up corner deletes everything instantly.

Right to withdraw — stop at any time, no questions asked.

Right to grievance — pramod.chaganti@icloud.com

What we do not do

No data sharing with any third party.

No advertising, commercial profiling, or behavioural monitoring.

No data retention after deletion is requested.

🔒 What this app does with data

    

      
📱
Child's entries live only on their device — never on our servers.

      
🧠
Each entry is sent to an AI (Anthropic Claude) to find interest patterns. Not stored, not logged beyond the device.

      
🚫
No ads, no analytics, no third-party sharing, no data selling. Ever.

      
🆘
If the AI detects distress signals in an entry, it shows the child helpline information instead of an interest analysis. The parent is not automatically notified.

      
🗑
"Erase all data" above wipes everything on this device instantly.
